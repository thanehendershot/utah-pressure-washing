What Affects Pricing?

Square Footage

Larger areas naturally require more time and cleaning solution, affecting the overall cost.

Surface Condition

Heavily soiled or stained surfaces require more time, specialized cleaners, and additional passes.

Surface Type

Delicate materials like wood or stucco may require soft washing techniques and gentle cleaners.

Accessibility

Hard-to-reach areas, steep terrain, or limited access can add time to the project.

Additional Services

Add-ons like sealing, staining preparation, or gutter cleaning affect the total price.

Location

Service areas farther from our base may include a small travel fee.
